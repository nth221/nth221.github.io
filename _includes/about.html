Welcome to the HAIL Research Group!

HAIL (Handong Artificial Intelligence Lab) is an active research group within the School of Computer Science and Electrical Engineering at Handong Global University. We are dedicated to pioneering advances in artificial intelligence, with research spanning deep learning, graph models, anomaly detection, and applied machine learning. Our mission is to drive innovation and excellence in the field of AI.
